Detroit Become Human

Detroit: Become Human puts the destiny of both mankind and androids in your hands, taking you to a near future where machines have become more intelligent than humans.

Recommended

23.1 hrs on record

POSTED: 14 SEPTEMBER

Review - An amazing game, with fantastic graphics, a great story and brilliant world building. It really feels like every choice you make does take you down a different path. The acting is great and I really enjoyed the relationships that grow between characters as they move through the story. I know that, as with a lot of these types of games, a lot of people will want to play this once to see 'their story' and not replay it but I found a lot of enjoyment in playing this through a second time by projecting different personalities on to the playable characters. I had two dramatically different experiences which for me sets this apart from its peers where sometimes you feel cheated in that you were only given the illusion of choice.
